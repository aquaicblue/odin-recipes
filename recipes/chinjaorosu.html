Chinjao Rosu

Description

Chinjao rosu is a Chinese-inspired dish that has become popular with homecooks across Japan.

Healthy and savory, this dish is easy to throw together in just a few minutes with a few simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • 300 grams thinly sliced beef
  • 2 green peppers
  • 100 grams bamboo shoot
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oystersauce
  • 2 cloves minced garlic

Directions

  1. Mix together your soy sauce and oyster sauce
  2. Thinly slice your peppers and bamboo shoots
  3. Add some neutral cooking oil to a pan, and turn on the heat
  4. Cook minced garlic in pan until fragrant, about one minute
  5. Add your peppers, and cook for another 2-3 minutes
  6. Remove peppers and garlic, and add a bit more oil to the pan
  7. Add your thinly sliced beef. Cook until no longer red, about 3 minutes
  8. Reintroduce your peppers and garlic to the pan
  9. Add the sauce mixture. Stir fry until sauce thickens
  10. Serve over a bed of rice, and enjoy!
