Lily Chan's Famous TKG

Description

You know it, you love it. This is the one and only Lily Chan's TKG.

For those who are unfamiliar, TKG stands for Tamago Kake Gohan

A classic Japanese meal with a Lily Chan twist! And what might that twist be?

We cook the egg. That's it. That's the twist.

The truth is I hate neba neba, so that's the only reason why I cook the egg. If you like raw eggs, maybe just do it the normal way? It's really up to you!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Kimchi
  • Soy Sauce

Directions

  1. Cook your egg over easy
  2. Place egg on top of hot rice
  3. Add toppings like kimchi and soy sauce
  4. Stir, and enjoy!
