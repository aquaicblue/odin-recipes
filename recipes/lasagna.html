Lasagna

,

Description

Ooey. Gooey. Delicious. This authentic Italian lasagna recipe is sure to wow your dinner guests, and satisfy your pasta cravings

Inexperienced cooks need not to fret; this recipe is incredibly simple, and only requires a few ingredients

What You'll Need

  • 6 Lasagna Sheets
  • 1 Cup Shredded Cheese
  • 1 Jar Marinara Sauce
  • 1 Tub of Cottage Cheese
  • Italian Spices of your choice

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta Sheets for 10 minutes
  2. Combine Your Cottage Cheese and Italian Spices
  3. Layer cooked Pasta Sheet, Sauce, Cheese, and repeat
  4. Sprinkle cheese over final layer
  5. Bake for 50 Minutes
  6. Enjoy!
